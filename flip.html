Our Services

Complete Plan Sets
We provide detailed code-compliant engineering drawings and documentation from 30% to IFC including PE stamps, required to obtain permits and guide accurate efficient construction execution.
Energy Production Modeling
Using industry-leading simulation tools such as PVSyst, PVCase, and Helioscope, we accurately forecast solar energy output. Our detailed energy models help assess project viability, optimize system design, and support financial modeling for stakeholders.
Feasibility Studies
We perform in-depth feasibility assessments for ground-mounted and rooftop solar installations across Commercial & Industrial (C&I) and utility-scale sectors. These studies include site analysis, environmental constraints, and cost-benefit analysis to determine project potential and return on investment.
Detailed Engineering & Design
Our team provides end-to-end Electrical, Civil and Structural, engineering services tailored for solar energy projects. From layout optimization to system interconnection, our designs adhere to the highest industry standards and align with applicable codes and regulations.
Power System Studies
We conduct a full range of Power System Studies to ensure the reliability, safety, and grid compliance of your solar project. Our studies include Short Circuit Analysis, Relay Coordination Studies and Arc Flash Studies. These evaluations help identify and mitigate potential system issues, enabling safe and efficient integration with utility grids and on-site power infrastructure.
Value Engineering
We apply value engineering principles to enhance system performance and reduce costs without compromising safety, reliability, or code compliance. Our goal is to deliver smart, efficient, and economically sound solutions that maximize project value.
Owner’s Engineer
Acting as the Owner’s Engineer, we provide technical oversight for the project owner. We provide independent oversight throughout the project lifecycle—verifying design integrity, construction quality, and regulatory compliance—while mitigating risks and supporting project success from concept to commissioning.
Expert Project Consulting
Our consulting services offer strategic and technical insight to guide your project from planning through execution. We help streamline processes, resolve complex challenges, and ensure timely delivery within scope and budget through customized advisory support.
