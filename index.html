About Us

At RadiantAmps Energy, we are passionate about transforming the energy landscape through innovation, precision, and integrity. Our core values Integrity, Excellence, Collaboration, and a deep-rooted Commitment to Renewable Energy guide every project we undertake.

With a proven track record of contributing to thousands of successful solar energy projects, our multidisciplinary team brings unmatched technical experience and project execution capability. Our engineers, designers and solar experts work seamlessly together to provide solutions that are code-compliant, accurate, and optimized for performance.

Our staff includes Professional Engineers (P.E.s) licensed in all 50 U.S. states, supported by a team of highly trained designers and project managers who understand both the permitting requirement and real-world field execution. We pride ourselves on being more than just a service provider. we aim to serve as a trusted extension of your project team.

Whether you're an owner, developer, EPC, contractor, RadiantAmps Energy delivers engineering excellence and support tailored to your specific needs. We minimize design errors, reduce permitting delays, and cut down unnecessary construction costs while helping you meet your renewable energy goals.

Our Services

RadiantAmps Energy offers a comprehensive suite of engineering services tailored to solar projects of every scale.

Energy Production Modeling

RadiantAmps Energy supports energy production modeling by leveraging tools like PVSyst, PVcase, and Helioscope to deliver accurate and bankable forecasts. We inform key design and equipment decisions, validate system performance, and provide inputs critical to financial models and investor confidence. By integrating modeling early in the project lifecycle, we help optimize energy yield, reduce uncertainty, and position projects for long-term success.

Feasibility Studies

RadiantAmps Energy conducts feasibility studies to evaluate the technical and financial viability of your projects. Our assessments cover site conditions, environmental constraints, interconnection potential, and cost-benefit analysis to determine project fit and maximize return on investment. By identifying risks and opportunities early, we help developers prioritize high-value sites and move forward with confidence.

Development Engineering

We get involved early by evaluating site conditions, creating conceptual designs, sizing equipment, and supporting permitting and utility coordination—ultimately preparing projects for success.

Detailed Engineering & Design

RadiantAmps Energy delivers electrical, civil, and structural engineering designs that meet the highest technical standards. Our detailed plans ensure regulatory compliance, support permitting and construction, and are tailored to optimize system performance and long-term reliability. We align every design with project goals, site conditions, and stakeholder requirements to enable seamless execution.

Designing Across All 50 States

RadiantAmps is licensed in all 50 states, enabling fast, compliant design anywhere in the U.S. We evaluate sites, model energy, and support local permitting from coast to coast.

Complete Plan Sets

RadiantAmps Energy delivers complete, code-compliant plan sets—including electrical, civil, and structural engineering documentation—at every project stage: 10%, 30%, 60%, up to Issued for Construction (IFC). Our drawings are prepared with precision, include PE stamps, and are tailored to enable accurate, efficient, and permit-ready project execution.

Value Engineering

RadiantAmps Energy applies value engineering to optimize system performance and reduce costs through strategic, data-driven design decisions. We identify opportunities for savings without compromising reliability, safety, or long-term durability—maximizing project value at every stage.

Power System Studies

RadiantAmps Energy performs comprehensive power system studies—including Short Circuit Analysis, Relay Coordination, Arc Flash Assessments—to ensure the safe, reliable integration of solar projects with the grid and on-site electrical systems. Our studies support compliance, protect personnel and equipment, and enhance overall system resilience.

Owner’s Engineer Services

RadiantAmps Energy provides independent Owner’s Engineer services, delivering expert oversight from design through commissioning. We rigorously validate quality, proactively manage risks, and ensure full compliance—safeguarding project success and protecting owner interests at every stage.

Expert Project Consulting

RadiantAmps Energy offers expert project consulting to navigate every phase—from planning and design to execution. We provide strategic guidance to resolve challenges, optimize workflows, and ensure projects stay on schedule and within scope.
