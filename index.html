About Us

At RadiantAmps Energy, we are passionate about transforming the energy landscape through innovation, precision, and integrity. Our core values Integrity, Excellence, Collaboration, and a deep-rooted Commitment to Renewable Energy guide every project we undertake.

With a proven track record of contributing to thousands of successful solar energy projects, our multidisciplinary team brings unmatched technical experience and project execution capability. Our engineers, designers and solar experts work seamlessly together to provide solutions that are code-compliant, accurate, and optimized for performance.

Our staff includes Professional Engineers (P.E.s) licensed in all 50 U.S. states, supported by a team of highly trained designers and project managers who understand both the permitting requirement and real-world field execution. We pride ourselves on being more than just a service provider. we aim to serve as a trusted extension of your project team.

Whether you're an owner, developer, EPC, contractor, RadiantAmps Energy delivers engineering excellence and support tailored to your specific needs. We minimize design errors, reduce permitting delays, and cut down unnecessary construction costs while helping you meet your renewable energy goals.

Our Services

RadiantAmps Energy offers a comprehensive suite of detailed engineering services tailored to solar projects of every scale. Our process-driven methodology ensures safe, code-compliant, and performance-optimized solutions.

Energy Production Modeling

Forecast energy output using top-tier tools like PVSyst, PVCase, and Helioscope. Our models inform design decisions and support financial assessments.

Feasibility Studies

Comprehensive assessments for C&I and utility-scale solar. Includes site, environmental, and cost-benefit analysis to evaluate potential and ROI.

Development Engineering

RadiantAmps Energy gets involved early in development engineering by evaluating site conditions, creating conceptual designs, performing energy modeling, and sizing key equipment to support accurate and competitive project bids. We help de-risk solar projects by ensuring code compliance, optimizing layouts, supporting permitting and utility coordination, and aligning technical scope with cost and financing requirements—ultimately preparing projects for successful execution.

Detailed Engineering & Design

Electrical, Civil, and Structural designs tailored to each site. Our plans follow strict standards and ensure regulatory compliance.

Designing Across All 50 States

RadiantAmps Energy is licensed to provide engineering services in all 50 states, enabling us to support solar projects nationwide with speed, precision, and full compliance with local codes. Our early involvement includes site evaluation, conceptual design, energy modeling, and equipment sizing to create accurate and competitive project bids. We help de-risk projects by ensuring technical and regulatory alignment, optimizing layouts, and supporting permitting and utility coordination—preparing projects for successful execution anywhere in the U.S.

Complete Plan Sets

Code-compliant electrical, civil, structural engineering plans and documentation from 10%, 30%, 60%, all the way to IFC, including PE stamps. Designed to ensure accurate, efficient, and permit-ready construction execution.

Value Engineering

Enhance performance and reduce costs through strategic design decisions—without compromising reliability or safety.

Power System Studies

Short Circuit, Relay Coordination, and Arc Flash Studies for safe, reliable integration with grid and on-site systems.

Owner’s Engineer Services

Independent project oversight from design through commissioning. We validate quality, manage risk, and ensure compliance every step of the way.

Expert Project Consulting

Guiding projects through planning, design, and execution. We help resolve challenges, streamline delivery, and keep you on track.
